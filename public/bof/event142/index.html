MUG Orphanage

Team Leader: LiGaYb

Nowadays, the realm of music game is ruled by few music genres. Hardcore(especially UK hardcore and happycore), special dnb(especially neurofunk and artcore) and trance(especially uplifting trance) covers over half of the MUG music. As a result, many players only concern about those genres. Some even said that only HDM are qualified for MUG; the harder a song is, the better quality it has. Meanwhile, some music creators also make lots of so-called MUGcore songs, which are very homogenized and boring to listen to - high bpm, high speed piano, boring melody, and a title which is hard to understand. Many composers also like to add a dubstep/color bass part into a song to cater MUG realm, but it totally doesn’t fit the whole song. As a house music lover, I(LiGaYb) could rarely find house music in music games, some games only include even 1 or 2 house music songs. In BOF venue, I also could rarely see house music, I only remembered 20- entries of house music in 400+ entries - most of them are hardcore/dnb/trance. This is the condition of house music, let alone other genres like glitch hop, garage, jungle, downtempo and those outside EDM like guitar, ancient style, pop and so on. I think the kernel of music game is to enjoy the music and how the chart matches the music, I’m not that enthusiastic about difficulty and chasing the limitation, I think every genre of music should have a place in MUG. This is why I set up this team. I also hope more interesting songs can appear in MUG, less so-called MUGcore can appear during the MUG development.
